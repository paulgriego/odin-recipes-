Tacos

What are tacos?

A taco is a traditional Mexican dish consisting of a small hand-sized corn or wheat tortilla topped with a filling. The tortilla is then folded around the filling and eaten by hand.

Ingredients

  • Taco Shells
  • Ground Beef
  • Cheese
  • Salsa

Steps to make tacos!

  1. Fry your ground beef!
  2. Prep your taco shells!
  3. Put your ground beef in the taco shell
  4. Top your ground beef with cheese!
  5. Add Salsa!
