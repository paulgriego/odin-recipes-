Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni

Steps to bake your pizza!

  1. Preheat your oven to 400 degrees
  2. Prepare your dough and form it into a circle
  3. Spread Tomato sauce on your dough in a even spread.
  4. Spread your cheese on top of your tomato sauce in even spread.
  5. Spread your Pepperoni on top of your cheese.
  6. Bake in the oven at 400 degrees for 25 minutes
